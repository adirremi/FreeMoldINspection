## Peace of mind for your home

Your home should be a safe place. Our **residential mold inspection** is designed for homeowners, buyers, and sellers who want certainty about indoor air quality and structural health. Whether you live in a single-family house, a condo, or an apartment, we tailor the inspection to your property and your concerns.

Mold is more than a cosmetic issue. It can affect the air your family breathes and, left unchecked, damage the materials that hold your home together. Because it so often hides behind walls, under floors, and in attics and crawl spaces, a professional inspection is the most reliable way to know what is really going on.

## When a residential inspection makes sense

Homeowners call us for many reasons, and all of them are valid:

- A persistent musty smell with no obvious source
- Visible staining, discoloration, or peeling paint
- A recent leak, flood, or roof problem
- Allergy or respiratory symptoms that improve when you leave the house
- Buying or selling a property and wanting an honest, independent evaluation

## Built around your family

We treat your home the way we would treat our own. Our inspectors are discreet, respectful, and focused on giving you straight answers — never a sales pitch. Our recommendations are honest and health-first, and because we are licensed for both mold assessment and remediation, we can also take care of mold removal from start to finish if you need it.

For buyers and sellers, a **pre-purchase or pre-sale inspection** is one of the smartest investments you can make. Buyers avoid inheriting hidden, costly problems, and sellers can address concerns proactively and move forward with confidence.

After the inspection, you receive a clear, documented report explaining what we found and what it means — so you can make confident decisions about the place your family calls home.
