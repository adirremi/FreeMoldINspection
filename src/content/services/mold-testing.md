## Know exactly what you are dealing with

A visual inspection tells you where mold is — **laboratory testing** tells you what it is. We collect samples from affected areas and submit them to an independent, accredited lab for analysis.

The results identify the specific mold species and the severity of the contamination, giving you and any remediation contractor an objective baseline to work from.
