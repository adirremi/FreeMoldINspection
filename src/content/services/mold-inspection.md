## Find the problem before it spreads

Mold rarely stays where you can see it. Our **comprehensive mold inspection** combines a trained eye with professional-grade tools to identify both visible growth and hidden colonies behind walls, under flooring, and inside HVAC systems.

We inspect kitchens, bathrooms, attics, crawl spaces, and any area where moisture collects. You receive a clear report with photos so you understand exactly what was found and what it means for your home or business.
