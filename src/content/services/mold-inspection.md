## Find the problem before it spreads

Mold rarely stays where you can see it. Our **comprehensive mold inspection** combines a trained eye with professional-grade tools to identify both visible growth and hidden colonies behind walls, under flooring, and inside HVAC systems. The goal is simple: give you a complete, honest picture of your property so you can make confident decisions.

We begin with a detailed conversation about your concerns — a musty smell, a recent leak, visible staining, or health symptoms that seem to flare up indoors. That context helps us focus the inspection where it matters most.

## What our inspection covers

During the visit, we methodically evaluate the areas where moisture and mold are most likely to develop, including:

- Kitchens, bathrooms, and laundry areas where humidity is high
- Attics, crawl spaces, and basements that are easy to overlook
- Around windows, doors, and rooflines where water can intrude
- HVAC systems and ductwork that can spread spores throughout the home
- Any location with a history of leaks or water damage

We use **thermal imaging** to reveal temperature differences that signal hidden moisture, and calibrated **moisture meters** to confirm what the camera suggests. This combination lets us find problems without unnecessary, destructive probing of your walls.

## Clear answers you can act on

When the inspection is complete, you receive a documented report with photos so you understand exactly what was found and why it matters. If mold is present, we explain the likely cause and the moisture source feeding it. If your property is clean, you get the peace of mind that comes from a thorough, independent evaluation.

Because we focus on inspection and testing rather than remediation, our findings stay objective. You will never feel pressured into expensive work you may not need.
